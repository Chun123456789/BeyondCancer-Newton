Our Objectives

Four goals guide every fundraiser, hospital visit, and awareness post we publish.

🔬
To research more about cancer & how today's medicine works

Research & Medical Insight

Provide students with important insights about medicine, giving our team a real understanding of the disease we're fighting to raise awareness about.

📣
To spread awareness about cancer

Awareness

Spread the message about how dangerous cancer can be, so more people recognise the signs early and understand why prevention and support matter.

🤝
To volunteer & raise funds for cancer patients

Volunteering & Fundraising

Volunteer and raise funds for helping cancer patients through hands-on activities and donations, including our work with the National Cancer Institute.

🧩
Team collaboration, across every department

Teamwork

Hone students' ability to work as a team, both within each department and between departments, so the whole organisation moves together.
